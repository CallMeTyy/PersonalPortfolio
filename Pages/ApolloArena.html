Apollo Arena


General Information

Summary: Apollo Arena is a multiplayer versus bow shooter game for Virtual Reality. Up to 8 players can battle each other in one of the two arenas to win the game. Different physics based arrows make the combat fun, quick and different!
Coding Platform: Unity
Target Platform: PCVR
Development Time: 8 weeks
Team (8 people):
- Artists: Jeroen Vedder, Kevin Nijkamp and Vy Nguyen
- Designers: Ira Evers, Jan de Weijer and Teun de Vries
- Engineers: Maurits Dijkman and Tycho Tuitert

Description & Contributions

This was a final project during my study where we were tasked to create a game suitable for a client. Our client was WirWar, a yearly event in Enschede, Netherlands where many people come to connect with companies and play the games they offer. Here, by proving to be the best project of our year, we represented Saxion and our study by showcasing our game to many others.

My Contributions
My focus during the project was on general gameplay, physics and networking. I worked on the following things:
- VR Physics (General + Arrow combinations)
- Bow & Arrow Physics
- Different arrow types and interactions
- World-UI integration
- Player IK (inverse kinematics) rig (hands & legs)
- Syncing everything over the network using mirror

This project was my introduction to mirror and my first multiplayer VR game. It taught me a lot about networking and the general implementation of VR interactable objects.
